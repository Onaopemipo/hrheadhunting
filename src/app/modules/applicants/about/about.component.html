About Us

We are Nigeria top job portal, that makes the recruiting process seamless for recruiters and job seekers alike. Job seekers can upload their video resumes and rate their resumes using Ai to get the best positions. Recruiters can upload psychometric and aptitude tests, filter key aspects of job seekers resumes, watch candidates video pitch; making short listing easy, cost and time saving. The portal has verified artisans in its offerings, making the hiring of technicians much easier. Trainers can also take advantage of the high traffic on the portal to advertise their training programs
We do not exclude artisans from our products. Today’s artisans are in high demand in Nigeria. We simply want to make the supply of manpower less demanding.
